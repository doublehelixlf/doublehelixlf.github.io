Welcome to Liu Lab

Our laboratory studies intrapatient tumor cell heterogeneity. We use experimental and computational technologies to molecularly define cancer cell subtypes in each patient. Our goal is to develop novel anti-cancer drugs and help clinicians design precision cancer therapy.

Our laboratory is part of the National Research Center for Translational Medicine (Shanghai). We are affiliated with Shanghai Jiao-Tong University School of Medicine and Ruijin Hospital.
