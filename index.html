Welcome to Liu Lab

Our laboratory studies tumor heterogeneity. We use experimental and computational technologies to determine the number mutations and cell types in each cancer patient. Our goal is to apply our work to discover new cancer drugs and help clinicians design precision cancer therapy.

Our laboratory is part of the National Research Center for Translational Medicine (Shanghai). We are affiliated with Shanghai Jiao-Tong University School of Medicine and Ruijin Hospital.
