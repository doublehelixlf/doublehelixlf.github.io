Welcome 欢迎

  Hello, welcome to the Laboratory of Cancer Epigenomics at the National Research Center for Translational Medicine (Shanghai). We are affiliated with Rui-Jin Hospital, Shanghai Jiao Tong University School of Medicine.

  Here we use experimental and computational technologies to molecularly define cancer cell subtypes in each patient. Our goal is to develop novel anti-cancer drugs and help clinicians design precision cancer therapy.
